##Add a badge to your site

> 640px x 64px

```html
<a href="http://stocktwits.com">
  <br />
  <img src="http://stocktwits.com/assets/badges/ST_64x64.png" style="width: 64px; height: 64px;" alt="I'm on StockTwits">
  <br />
</a>
```
